🌿 Our Purpose

"As the Women of Essence, our goal is to bring hope and meaning to women everywhere; to empower women through workshops, group sessions, and other interactive methods; to plan church and community activities where women can experience meaningful fellowship, joy and friendship that comes from being loved by others."

🌎 Our Vision

To continue to expand the boundaries of our Women’s Ministry beyond the limits of our local church into our wider community, bringing empowerment, hope and healing to women in their various conditions of life.

🎯 Our Mission

To impact the lives of other women by empowering them toward purpose and wholeness.
